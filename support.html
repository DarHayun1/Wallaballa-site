Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new shopping list?

Tap the "+" button on the main screen to create a new list.

Can I share my shopping list with others?

Yes, you can share your lists by tapping the share icon and entering the email address of the person you want to share with.

How do I delete items from my list?

Swipe left on any item to delete it, or tap the checkbox to mark it as complete.
