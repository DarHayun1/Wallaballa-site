Frequently Asked Questions

Can I share my shopping list with others?

Yes, you can share your lists by tapping the share icon and sending the link to your friends, or send as text from the list menu.

How do I delete my account?

Open the profile page from the home page menu and click the "Delete Account" button.

How do I create a new shopping list?

Tap the "+" button on the main screen to create a new list. You can then give your list a name and start adding items right away.
